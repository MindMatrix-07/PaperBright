# PaperBright Design

PaperBright follows a Vercel-inspired product interface: black and white precision, tight spacing, clear borders, compact controls, and restrained typography.

## Principles

- The first screen is the working text editor, not a landing page.
- Use paper-like surfaces with crisp ink, neutral borders, and one restrained blue signal color.
- Keep toolbars compact and predictable.
- Use Lucide icons for action buttons.
- Avoid decorative hero sections, gradients, and card-heavy marketing composition.
- Make the editor comfortable for long writing sessions on desktop and mobile.

## Product Shape

- Header: product identity, editable document title, document actions.
- Editor: focused writing surface with formatting controls and local save state.
- Sidebar: live stats, scratch notes, and session metadata.
- Mobile: stacked layout with the editor first and metadata below.
